Auto Hide Bones automatically hides bones during animation playback and G/R/S transforms in Pose Mode, so you can focus on your animation without visual clutter.

This add-on does not modify Blender's built-in playback or transforms. It registers new operators with auto-hide functionality. For the best experience, we recommend assigning your usual playback hotkey (e.g. Space) through Addon Preferences.

### Features
- **Auto Hide on Play**: Hides bones when playing animation, restores on stop
- **Auto Hide on Transform**: Hides bones during G/R/S operations
- **Toggle** (Alt+C): Enable/disable auto-hide with a single toggle (applies to both playback and transform)

### Usage
1. Enter Pose Mode
2. Press Alt+C to enable auto-hide (or toggle from the viewport header)
3. Press Space to play or G/R/S to transform — bones hide automatically
