Auto Hide Bones automatically hides bones during animation playback and G/R/S transforms in Pose Mode, so you can focus on your animation without visual clutter.

This add-on does not modify Blender's built-in playback or transforms. It registers new operators with auto-hide functionality. For the best experience, we recommend assigning them to the same hotkeys (e.g. Space, G, R, S) through Addon Preferences.

### Features
- **Auto Hide on Play**: Hides bones when playing animation, restores on stop
- **Auto Hide on Transform**: Hides bones during G/R/S operations
- **Individual toggles**: Enable/disable each feature independently

### Usage
1. Enter Pose Mode
2. Enable auto-hide:
   - Viewport header: G/R/S transform auto-hide
   - Timeline footer: Playback auto-hide
3. Press Space to play or G/R/S to transform — bones hide automatically
